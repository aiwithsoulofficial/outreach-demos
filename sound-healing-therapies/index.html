About Faith

Faith Challinor-Wheatley

Faith's journey with sound began in the Welsh choirs of the 1960s, competing at the Llangollen International Musical Eisteddfod as a young singer. After decades working in holistic therapies - opening her first clinic in Swansea in 1976 - a near-fatal road accident in 1998 changed everything.

The accident damaged her vocal cords, and her recovery journey led her deeper into the healing power of sound and vibration. What began as personal healing became her life's work - developing Light Melodics, a registered therapeutic technique that combines aromatic oils with intuitive vocal healing.

Today Faith is one of Wales' most experienced sound healing practitioners, holding memberships with the College of Sound Healing, the Sound Healers Association, and the International Council of Holistic Therapists.
