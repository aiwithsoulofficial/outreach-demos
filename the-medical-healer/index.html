The Healer

Johnny Batterson

Nearly two decades ago, Johnny was bombarded by the Spiritual Realm - faces in the backs of cars, in the trees, on his work machinery. After six months of relentless contact, he finally gave in and asked what they wanted. The answer was clear: become a healer.

In 2018, his Spirit Guides urged him to perform a healing on a family friend with an inoperable brain tumor. During an eight-hour drive to the Sunshine Coast, 39 High Powered Spirit Guides introduced themselves in a profound ceremony. The healing was a success - and Johnny knew his purpose was real.

Today he works with clients across Australia and internationally, channeling targeted energy healing for conditions from chronic pain to terminal illness. He works as a vessel - not through force or personal will, but through alignment with a higher power.
