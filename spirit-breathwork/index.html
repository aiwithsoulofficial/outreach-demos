FAQ

Do I need prior breathwork experience?

A personal breathwork practice is recommended but not required. The pre-retreat online portal will help you prepare regardless of your current experience level.

What if I can't make these dates?

If you need to transfer your booking to a future retreat, this is possible with an admin fee. Spots are non-refundable but transferable.

Is accommodation shared or private?

Standard accommodation is twin share at Komune Resort. Single rooms are available at an additional cost - just mention this when booking your call.

What happens after the training?

You'll leave as a certified Level 1 Spirit Breathwork Facilitator with the tools, confidence, and business knowledge to start facilitating immediately. Ongoing community support is included.

Can I pay in instalments?

Payment plans are available. Book a call with the Spirit team to discuss options that work for your situation.

Ready to Begin?

Spaces are limited. Book a call with the Spirit team to secure your spot and ask any questions.
